Welcome! I am a PhD candidate in **Management Information Systems** at the <span style="color: #0066cc;">[School of Management and Economics](https://sme.bit.edu.cn/), [Beijing Institute of Technology](https://www.bit.edu.cn/)</span>, Beijing, China. My research lies at the intersection of **healthcare informatics** and **artificial intelligence**, with a particular focus on leveraging advanced computational methods to address critical challenges in **mental health support** and **online healthcare delivery**.

Research Interests
======
My research encompasses several interconnected areas within AI-enabled healthcare:

* **Mental Health Informatics**: Developing empathetic dialogue systems and intelligent agents for mental health support, with emphasis on emotional intelligence and therapeutic communication
* **Reinforcement Learning for Healthcare**: Designing multi-agent reinforcement learning frameworks to optimize decision-making in complex healthcare scenarios
* **Large Language Models in Healthcare**: Investigating the application of LLMs for empathetic response generation, patient support, and clinical decision assistance
* **Digital Health Platforms**: Exploring the dynamics of online health communities, telemedicine systems, and social support networks
* **Healthcare Data Analytics**: Applying advanced data mining and machine learning techniques to extract actionable insights from healthcare data
