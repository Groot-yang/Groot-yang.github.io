Welcome! I am a PhD candidate in Information Management and Information Systems at [Beijing Institute of Technology](https://www.bit.edu.cn/), Beijing, China.

Research Interests
======
My research focuses on the intersection of healthcare informatics and artificial intelligence. Specifically, I am interested in:

* **Online Healthcare**: Exploring digital health platforms and telemedicine systems
* **Data Mining**: Extracting meaningful patterns and insights from healthcare data
* **Reinforcement Learning**: Applying RL techniques to healthcare decision-making problems
* **Large Language Models (LLMs)**: Investigating the applications of LLMs in healthcare and information systems

About This Site
======
This website showcases my academic work, including publications, research projects, and other professional activities. Feel free to explore and reach out if you're interested in collaboration or have any questions about my research.
