## Abstract

In this presentation, we introduce an Emotional Intelligence-enhanced multi-agent reinforcement learning framework for probing and improving empathetic dialogue generation in mental health support scenarios. Our approach leverages emotional intelligence principles to enhance the empathetic capabilities of conversational AI systems, particularly in sensitive mental health contexts.
